Day 2
Hardiwar - Rishikesh - Mussoorie (2 Nights)
Today we visit Prati Badri Kedarnath Temple - a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, Lakshman Mandir - place where Lakshman meditated to attain enlightenment, Lakshman Zhulla - a famous hanging bridge across the river Ganges. Later, we take a thrilling experience of White Water Rafting at Rishikesh. Further we proceed to Mussoorie - also known as Queen of the Hills.
Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Tea/Coffee after Rafting, Tea/Coffee and one Dry Snack en-route to Mussoorie
Day 3
Mussoorie

Today we see the beautiful Kempty Falls, which offers a mesmerizing panoramic view as the water falls from the mountains. Later we enjoy Ropeway Ride at Gun Hill point- a favourite place for viewing picturesque Himalayan range. At Gun Hill point you can photograph yourself in a Pahadi outfit at your own cost. In the evening we have some free time for shopping.

Note: Please carry a swim-suit while visiting Kempty falls

Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Tea/coffee/Soft drink and Bhutta at Kempty falls, Noodles at Gun Hill point and Tea/Coffee at Kulri market
Day 4
Mussoorie - Nainital (3 Nights)
Today we proceed to Nainital -Commonly known as the Lake District of India. Upon arrival we have free time to relax and enjoy the surroundings.
Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Today we proceed to Nainital -Commonly known as the Lake District of India. Upon arrival we have free time to relax and enjoy the surroundings.
Day 5
Nainital
Today we take a Cable car to experience Snow view point which offers a panoramic view of the mighty Himalayas. Followed by a visit to Hanumangarhi temple - one of the most popular temples of Lord Hanuman and Chidiyaghar Zoo - a high altitude zoo situated in Nainital.
Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Sukhi Bhel, Buransh juice at Snow View Point, Tea/Coffee in the evening

Day 6
Nainital
Today we visit Prati Badri Kedarnath Temple - a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, Lakshman Mandir - place where Lakshman meditated to attain enlightenment, Lakshman Zhulla - a famous hanging bridge across the river Ganges. Later, we take a thrilling experience of White Water Rafting at Rishikesh. Further we proceed to Mussoorie - also known as Queen of the Hills.
Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Momos and one Dry Snack at Mall road
Day 7
Nainital - Jim Corbett (2 Nights)
Today we proceed to Jim Corbett– known for its glory of being India's oldest and most prestigious National Park. En route we visit Jim Corbett Museum at Kaladungi.
Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Shikanji Sharbat at Kaladungi, one Dry Snack en-route to Corbett Park, Tea/Coffee at Corbett Park
Day 8
Jim Corbett Park
Today we enjoy Jeep safari adventure of wildlife viewing in the region of 'Man eaters of Kumaon'. You may spot the Royal Bengal Tiger, Deer, Indian Hog Deer, Sambar Deer, Otter, Asiatic Black Bear, Sloth Bear, etc. In the evening, we take a Nature walk in the Corbett Park.
Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Tea/Coffee during Safari and Nature Walk

Day 9
Corbett Park – Delhi – Departure

Today we proceed to Delhi. It’s time now to say goodbye to our travel companions. Let’s stay in touch with each other through email, phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and meet again on yet another memorable tour. See you all!!

Tour Guests will proceed to Delhi airport as per their schedule flight and start their return journey back home.

Tour Concludes.

Tea/Coffee and two Dry Snacks en-route to Delhi
